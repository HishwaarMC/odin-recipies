Pizza Recipie

What we will be creating

Wel will need the following ingredients in order to make the dish:

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
Now, its time to start baking!


Cooking Instructions

  1. Kneed the dough into a flat disk
  2. Applly the sauce evenly
  3. Apply the cheese evenly
  4. Apply the pepperoni throughout the pizza
  5. Put into oven for 20 minutes at 230 degrees
  6. Enjoy
