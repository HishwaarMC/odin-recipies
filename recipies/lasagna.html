Lasagna Recipie

What we will be creating

Wel will need the following ingredients in order to make dish:

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Beef
Now, its time to start cooking!


Cooking Instructions

  1. Mix the pasta into a fine paste, and apply it to the bottom of the pan
  2. Mix the sauce with the cheese and beef
  3. Apply the mixture onto the pasta paste
  4. Repeat the previous steps two more times
  5. Put into oven for 60 minutes at 230 degrees
  6. Enjoy

Congratulations, you have created a delicious lasagna for you and your family to enjoy!
