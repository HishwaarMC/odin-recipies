Burger Recipie

What we will be creating

Wel will need the following ingredients in order to make the dish:

  • Buns
  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • pickles
Now, its time to start stacking!


Cooking Instructions

  1. Place the buns on the table
  2. put the patty on the bottom bun
  3. put the cheese on the patty
  4. Put the pickles on the cheese
  5. Put the top bun on the pickles
  6. Enjoy

Congratulations, you have created a delicious burger for you and your family to enjoy!
